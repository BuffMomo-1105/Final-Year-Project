Lentil - दाल

Lentil(दाल) is grown in the Terai, Inner Terai and mid-hills and constitutes 60% of the total grain legume area and production. Lentil is the fourth most important cool season crop of Nepal shares 60% in area and 65% in production of total legumes.

Sugarcane - उखु

Sugarcane is the largest commercial cash crop cultivated in Nepal. Out of Nepal's 75 districts, five (Mahottari, Sarlahi, Rautahat, Nawalparasi and Kapilbastu) are the major sugarcane cultivation districts.

Barley - जौ

Barley is one of the major food crops in high hill region of Nepal. Naked barley is a traditional, climate-resilient and highly nutritious crop of the high mountains of Nepal.

Coffee is grown in 42 districts in Nepal. Gulmi, Arghakhanchi, Pyuthan, Palpa and Syangja are the largest coffee producers. In addition, coffee plantation is expanding as commercial farming in 21 districts of mid-hill areas of central and western region.
